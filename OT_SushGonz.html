Sushin Gonzales Mexico/Japon

This mural, created for the Mexican/Japanese fusion restaurant Sushin Gonzales, aims to represent the duality of the two cultures through various characteristic elements and their equivalents in each culture. It features papel picado on the Mexican side and paper lanterns on the Japanese side, jacaranda and cherry blossoms, Quetzalcoatl and a Japanese dragon, the molcajete and sushi. The restaurant combines Japanese sushi with Mexican fillings.
